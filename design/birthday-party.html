â“ Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about hosting a Creative Stage birthday party!

Where can we hold the party?

We come to you! We can set up in your home, backyard, community center, or any indoor space. We can also host parties at our Creative Stage studio for an additional $50.

What ages work best for art parties?

Our parties are designed for ages 4-12, with activities adapted for the specific age group. Mixed age groups work great too - we adjust projects so everyone can participate successfully.

How much space do we need?

We need about 8x10 feet of table space for 8 children, with additional space for setup. Indoor or covered outdoor spaces work best. We bring tables if needed (+$25).

What about the mess?

Zero mess for parents! We bring table covers, aprons for all guests, and handle complete cleanup. All materials are washable and child-safe.

Can we customize the activities?

Absolutely! We love incorporating your child's favorite colors, characters, or interests. Just let us know preferences when booking and we'll create something special.

How far in advance should we book?

We recommend booking 2-4 weeks in advance, especially for weekend parties. However, we can sometimes accommodate last-minute requests with 48 hours notice.

What's included in the pricing?

All art supplies, decorations, instruction, setup, cleanup, take-home artwork, and digital photos are included. You only need to provide the space and refreshments.

What if a guest has allergies or special needs?

Please let us know in advance! All our materials are non-toxic, but we can accommodate specific allergies and adapt activities for children with special needs.
